I. Design Exercises

Excerpts of design exercises done to strengthen hard skills and learn what I might not come across outside of school.

II. Confucius Institute

Print design for literature and art events organized by the Confucius Institute at the University of Waterloo.

III. Orientation Week

As part of a collaborative course project, my classmates and I prototyped a redesign of the University of Waterloo's orientation week. We created a website that would allow students to design their own orientation experience.

IV. Mind Maps

Mind Maps created as part of university coursework, organizing tips on essay writing and information from readings.

V. Shirts & Hoodies

I designed apparel in high school for school productions. Each show had a unifying theme and the shirts and hoodies would be worn by cast members and staff to promote the show.
