Rethinking Orientation Week

As part of a class at the University of Waterloo, we were tasked with coming up with a way to reduce student stress by redesigning the university's orientation week. To do this, we created a website that would allow incoming students to organize their week online. For this assignment, my main roles were designing and building the working website prototype, shown below.

Note: The photos used in the website prototype are placeholder images that belong the University of Waterloo.

Create Your Own Schedule

In the current orientation week, you receive a schedule on the first day that tells your where to go and what to do. We wanted to let students decide what activities they wanted to do ahead of time based on their own personal interests. In the redesign, students can create their own schedule and access it online at any time.

Provding Helpful Information

The website redesign also provides additional information explaining the purpose of orientation week and why students should attend. As it can be a confusing time, we also added a page of frequently asked questions to help students find answers quickly.
